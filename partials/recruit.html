Why go greek?

Membership in a Greek letter organization is a lifetime obligation. Choosing the Greek community means working with a group of men and women who can exchange and stand for common goals and ideas and being held to a higher standard than other college students. The Greek experience during the college years is a gateway to many rewards and connections later in life. Membership will be an unwritten bond of friendship no matter what course a young man's life takes.
